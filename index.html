Getting here

Venue & Travel

Gaynes Park is a stunning country estate set in the heart of the Essex countryside — well worth the journey.

🚂 By Train

Epping station (Central Line / Elizabeth line interchange) is the nearest stop, approximately 10 minutes by taxi or rideshare from the venue.

🚗 By Car

Gaynes Park is easily reached via the M11 or M25. Free parking is available on site. Please plan ahead for your return journey if you're drinking!

🏨 Accommodation

We recommend booking early. There are several hotels and B&Bs in Epping town centre, a short taxi ride away. We'll add a recommended list here soon.
